Our Mission

Universal Translator was created to break down language barriers, especially for African languages that are often underrepresented in translation tools. We believe that everyone should be able to communicate in their native language while connecting with the global community.

Technology

Our platform combines multiple translation approaches including custom dictionaries for African languages, machine learning models, and community-contributed translations to provide the most accurate results possible.
